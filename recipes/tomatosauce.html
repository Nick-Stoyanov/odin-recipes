San Marzano Tomato Sauce

Using the best tomatoes you can buy is a crutial part of making the best tomato sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 2 kg San Marzano tomatoes, peeled
  • Olive oil
  • 3-4 Garlic cloves
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Sautee thinly sliced garlic in olive oil for a minute.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes.
  3. Salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cook for 20 minutes on high heat.
  5. Enjoy!
