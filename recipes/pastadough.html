Egg Pasta Dough

My favorite Egg pasta dought recipe. Ideal for stuffed pastas, lasagna sheets or Fettucini.

Ingredients:

  • 450g Tipo 00 flour
  • 120g Semolina
  • 330g eggs
  • 95g egg yolks
  • 1/2 T olive oil
  • pinch salt

Steps

  1. Sift flours.
  2. Combine all dry ingredients.
  3. Whisk eggs well, add olive oil.
  4. Mix flour with eggs.
  5. Knead until surface is smooth and gluten is developed.
  6. Wrap and rest for an hour in the fridge.
  7. Ready to roll out!
